Summary and Recommendations

Of the 23 fields analyzed, 11 are merge fields (N/A for auto-fill purposes). Of the remaining 12 user/installer input fields, 8 are suitable for full or conditional auto-population, and 4 require manual input. This yields an effective auto-fill coverage rate of approximately 67% of actionable fields.

The primary barrier to broader automation is the category of fields requiring active customer acknowledgment or live signatures, which are non-automatable by design.

Recommended approach: implement auto-fill for the 8 eligible fields as a pre-population step, with a final human review checkpoint before the form is presented to the customer for signature.
